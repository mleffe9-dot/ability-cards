UNNOVA

Wither Aspect
Fragment Slots (2)
Everything rots eventually.
Shooting at a drench portal or a pool will teleport you to its location erupting any nearby pools.

HOLY GROUND

Wither Aspect
Fragment Slots (2)
A forgotten chalice.
Casting your rift nearby enemies will absorb their essence causing it to become a swelling rift.
The strength increases based on nearby enemies.

JETSTREAM

Wither Aspect
Fragment Slots (2)
A mysterious candle burning with liquid.
Hold your melee input to release a continous torrent of wither energy.
Dealing sustained damage will apply drain. Hitting terrain with the ability will paint the area with pools.

HYNM OF DESCRATION

Wither Aspect
Fragment Slots (2)
Sing the song of desecration and be forgotten.
Your wither grenades gain additional effects.
Drench grenade- adds a third portal to the throw, and increases the damage of the rain
Fester grenade- your fester grenade deals more damage the longer it stays dormant before exploding
Eruption grenade- nearby eruptions return eruption grenade energy
